ABOUT

Cindy Weng

From Hsinchu City, Taiwan
Graduated from Wuling High School in Taoyuan City
Studying in National Chiao Tung University

I love theater performances. Whenever I stand on stage and play different roles, I can experience a different life. I think this is the most fascinating part of drama. In addition, every successful performance always gives me a full sense of accomplishment. I like this feeling of being wholeheartedly devoted to the things I love.

I hope that I can learn about the various cultures in the world by learning languages, so that I can really understand the characters and uniqueness of various nations, so that I can show their stories more truthfully.
